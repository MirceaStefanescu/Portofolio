# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge
We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Being respectful and considerate
- Focusing on collaboration and constructive feedback
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks or trolling
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and enforcing this code.

## Reporting
Report issues to the maintainers via the project repository.
